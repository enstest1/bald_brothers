Bröther Commandment #1

𝕿𝖍𝖔𝖚 𝖘𝖍𝖆𝖑𝖙 𝕽𝖊𝖘𝖕𝖊𝖈𝖙 𝖙𝖍𝖊 𝕭𝖆𝖑𝖉.

The bare crown is sacred. Let no wig, cap, or illusion defile it. From the bald head springs all truth.
